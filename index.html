About Great Heights University

The Great Heights University (GHU) is a pioneering Seventh-day Adventist institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence, ethical leadership, and holistic development. With a diverse range of degree programs offered both online and at the Chegutu campus, GHU aims to address critical skill gaps, drive economic growth, and enhance healthcare services. Through innovative education, strategic infrastructure development, and collaborative partnerships, GHU is poised to become a catalyst for positive change in Zimbabwe and beyond
